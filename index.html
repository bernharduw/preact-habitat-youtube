preact-habitat is a 1.6kb module that will help you ship your Preact components to any world wide DOM page in a very easy and neat way.

What's new in v1.0.0:
✨ Pass props from client's HTML to your components
✨ Bundle multiple independent widgets and mount them seperatly
✨ Mount multiple clones of a widget with different props with new `data-mount` attribute
